OctoDraw Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24 February 2026

OctoDraw ("we", "our", "us") provides lottery result tools, number checking, and nearby outlet lookup.

1) Data We Collect

We do not require account sign-up and we do not collect your name, email, or phone number.

The app may access:

  • Location (Precise Location): used only to show nearby outlets and map directions.
  • Camera / Photos (optional): used only when you choose ticket scanning/checking features.
  • Notifications (optional): used to send result alerts if you enable notifications.

2) How We Use Data

  • Show latest 4D/TOTO results
  • Check ticket numbers
  • Find nearest outlets
  • Send result notifications

3) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

The app may send requests to third-party services only to provide functionality, such as official/public result sources and map/geocoding services.

4) Data Storage and Retention

  • Most app data is processed in real time.
  • Basic app preferences and last-seen draw IDs may be stored on your device.
  • We do not maintain user profiles for this app.

5) Your Choices

You can control permissions at any time in iOS Settings: Location, Camera, Photos, Notifications. If disabled, related features may not work.

6) Children’s Privacy

OctoDraw is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7) Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
